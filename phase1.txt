Step 2 — FastAPI Scheduler

Your FastAPI backend runs a job every minute.

Example logic:

Check contacts where birthday = today
Generate scheduled messages
Push to Redis

Example SQL:

SELECT *
FROM contacts
WHERE EXTRACT(MONTH FROM birthday) = EXTRACT(MONTH FROM NOW())
AND EXTRACT(DAY FROM birthday) = EXTRACT(DAY FROM NOW());

Step 4 — Go Agent Worker

Your Go agent listens to Redis.

Flow:

Redis queue
   ↓

   ↓
